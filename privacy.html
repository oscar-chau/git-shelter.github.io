Privacy Policy for Caritas

The Caritas Development Team operates the Caritas platform, including web and mobile applications. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

What Data Do We Collect?

  • Personal identification information (name, email address)
  • User account data (authentication via Clerk)
  • Participant case information entered by authorized staff
  • Usage data (basic interaction with the application)

How Do We Collect Your Data?

We collect data when you:

  • Register or log in to the application
  • Enter or update participant case information
  • Use the platform features such as scheduling or reporting
  • Provide feedback via email or forms

How Will We Use Your Data?

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Caritas platform
  • Manage user accounts and authentication
  • Store and manage participant case records
  • Improve application functionality and user experience
  • Respond to feedback or support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be processed by third-party services required to operate the platform, including:

  • Clerk (authentication services)
  • MongoDB Atlas (database hosting)

How Do We Store Your Data?

Your data is securely stored using cloud-based services such as MongoDB Atlas. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including access controls and secure authentication.

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the operation of the platform or as required for the project. Data may be deleted upon request or when no longer needed.

Marketing

We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to processing of your data

If you make a request, we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to support authentication and improve user experience.

Third-Party Websites

Our platform may contain links to external websites. This privacy policy applies only to Caritas. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
